  • Process of BITS Mail and Coursera Account Setup

    • Log in to BITS mail through Gmail
    • Sign up for Coursera using your BITS Google account
    • Wait for the configuration email to arrive in your BITS mailbox
    • Configure and complete the orientation
    • Add a personal account by navigating to the settings
    • Congratulations! Setup is complete

    When will we get our student ID card?

    • When classes start, after a few weeks

  • When will we receive our email ID or student ID?

    • If you received a provisional letter, get a printout, sign, and submit it.
    • Submit a government ID card.
    • Pay the fee.
    • Generally, it will take 10 to 15 days to receive the email or student ID.

  • Some questions regarding transcripts:

    • If you are a CBSE student, then you don't have to submit transcripts.
    • In my case, I used a mark sheet in place of transcripts because it is required.
    • Do not leave it empty; you will get an error.
    • Foreign students need an AIU Certificate.
